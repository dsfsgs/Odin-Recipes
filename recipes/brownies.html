y Brownies

Brownies

Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well. They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Ingredients

  • For the brownies: butter, white sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cocoa powder, all-purpose flour, salt, and baking powder
  • For the frosting: , cocoa powder, honey, vanilla extract, confectioners' sugar

How to Make Homemade Brownies

  1. Make the brownies: the butter on the stove, then remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients. Beat in the dry ingredients. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
  2. Frost the brownies: the frosting ingredients together until well-combined, then frost the brownies while they are still warm. Cut into squares when cool.
